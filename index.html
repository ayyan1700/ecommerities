About

Founded in 2019, E-Commerce Metier Ventures was built on a simple yet powerful vision: to empower online sellers and businesses with the right strategies, systems, and support to scale efficiently. With over six years of experience in the service industry, we’ve helped numerous clients streamline their operations through tailored solutions in marketplace management, wholesale distribution, product sourcing, and private label brand growth.

Our journey accelerated in 2021 with the launch of our Upwork agency, marking a milestone that connected us with global clients and long-term partnerships. Since then, we’ve expanded our expertise into social media marketing, media buying, and strategic brand scaling, ensuring every business we work with experiences measurable growth and lasting impact.
